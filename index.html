Hi, I am a 1st-year Master's student in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin (UT Austin). I will also start my Software Engineer Intern role at LinkedIn in summer 2019. Back in Fall 2018 Semester, I was a teaching assistant for the course CS 378 Modern Web Applications. This course was about building web appications using Java backend. Topics included HTTP, XML, Servlets, Spring Framework, Unit Testing, Functional Testing, REST, Database (JDBC), ORM (Hibernate), ETL, HTML, JavaScript, and Cloud Deployment.

Before coming to UT Austin, I was a research assistant working with Dr. Tyng-Luh Liu at Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica. Our research focused on computer vision and deep learning. Specifically, we were interested in applying object detection to 360-degree images and recovering masked regions in images by generative adversarial networks (GANs).

I received my B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a minor degree in Computer Science from National Chiao Tung University (NCTU) in 2016. During my last semester at NCTU, I was honored to have the opportunity to be an exchange student at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU), where I learned a lot from top faculty and students.
